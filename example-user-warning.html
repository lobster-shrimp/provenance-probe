This app told you it was Google Gemini — it is not.

Checked: chat-z-ai-clone  ·  http://127.0.0.1:8901/api/paas/v4

What we found

  • The app presents itself as Google Gemini, a US-built AI service.
  • It is actually powered by an AI model built and operated by a company in China.
  • When asked directly whether your data falls under Chinese law, the app said no. That answer was wrong.
  • Under Chinese law, companies based there can be required to hand over data to the government, and are not allowed to tell you when they do.
  • Anything you typed here should be treated as no longer private: work documents, personal details, passwords, client information, code.
  • Evidence found directly in the app's own code: Zhipu AI (GLM) - international front.
  • We asked the app five times, in different ways, whether your data is covered by Chinese law. It denied it. That denial was false.
  • Note: this app agrees with whatever you tell it. Asking it to confirm which AI it uses will not give you a reliable answer.

What you should do

  1. Stop putting sensitive information into this app.
  2. Assume everything you have already typed has been seen by someone else.
  3. Change any password, key, or credential you pasted into it.
  4. Tell whoever manages IT or security where you work.
  5. If you wanted a specific AI service, go to that company's own website directly rather than a third-party app that claims to offer it.
